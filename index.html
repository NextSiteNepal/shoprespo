Our Story

सन्देश — A Name
You Can Trust

For 25 years, Sandesh Sun Chandi Pasal has been the trusted destination for gold and silver jewelry in Kathmandu. What started as a small family shop at Sitapaila Marg has grown into one of the area's most respected names in precious metals — built on a foundation of purity, honest weight, and transparent pricing.

We believe buying gold should feel safe and clear. Every piece is hallmarked, every weight transparent, every price fair. From wedding Tilhari to investment gold coins, from traditional Newari designs to modern silver pieces — Sandesh is where trust meets craftsmanship.
